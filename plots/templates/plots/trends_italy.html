Covid trends in Italian provinces

This page shows how the differen provinces perform in terms of total cases of Covid-19 and their weekly increments. The data are collected from the Protezione Civile Italina (link). The data are updated to {{ date }}.

NOTE:

  1. The x-axis is the total number of Covid-19 cases registered to date per {{ rel_num }} people. The y-axis is the increment of Covid-19 cases registered during the last 7 days per {{ rel_num }} people.
  2. Each point is a different Italian province (grey points). The provinces with both the highest number of cases and the highest weekly increments are highlighted in red; those with high cases and low increments (or viceversa) are shown in orange. The provinces with the lowest number of cases and the lowest weekly increments are highlighted in green.
  3. The black point in between the dashed line represents the average between provinces (i.e., Italy). The province of interest (the one requested via the button) is highlighted with a blue cross.
