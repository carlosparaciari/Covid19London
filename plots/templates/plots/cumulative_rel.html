Comparison between cases in London boroughs

This page allows to compare the cumulative cases of Covid19 in different London boroughs. The number of cases are taken relative to 100,000 people; to normalise the cases the 2018 census has been used, which provides an estimate of the boroughs' population. Rather than showing the cases by date, we show them by days passed since 1 relative case ocurred. The data are collected from the governative website https://coronavirus.data.gov.uk. The data are updated to {{ date }}.
